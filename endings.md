# Case Endings


## Nouns

|  | Nominative | Prepositional | Accusative |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Masculine | consonant/ь | е/и | - (animate: а/я) |
| Neutral    | о/е | е | - |
| Feminine  | а/я/ь | е/и | у/ю |
| Plural    | ы/и (neutral: а/я) | ах/ях | inanimate: -; animate: consonant: ов/ев, жчшщь: ей, аяое: - |
